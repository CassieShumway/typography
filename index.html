Hello Heirarchy

Follow along as you read through

Notice how your eyes easily scan through? As you are reading, you can see the clear differentiation between the title, headline, and body of text. Using italics, font weight, and varying font sizes all help towards happy hierarchy.

Also, look for typefaces that have a large selection of family fonts. All the fonts you see here are within the Apolline typeface. This makes for an easy mix that will always be balanced and well designed.

Serif

This is a serif typeface.

It has flourishes (that extend) at the end of its strokes. Serif typefaces are classic, formal and elegant.

Sans Serif

This is a sans serif typeface.

It has no flourishes at the end of its strokes. Sans serif typefaces are modern, clean, and minimal.
